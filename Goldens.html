The golden color is the color of syrian hamsters in the wild.  Those in the wild are also short haired.  Long hair is a more recent mutation.  The golden color is sometimes also called "agouti."

This is one of the more common and popular colors.  They are easy to breed since many pairings can produce some or all goldens.
