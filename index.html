Above all, I am a hamster hobbyist. I breed for temperament, but I also breed
for show qualities. I breed Dwarf and Syrian hamsters and live in Southern California.
My current focus is on understanding and documenting the color genetics
of the Campbells dwarf hamsters.  I am the President and Chief Judge
for the California Hamster Association (CHA).
