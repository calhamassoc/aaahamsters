Watching a Large Syrian Litter Grow
Page 2
This litter of syrians was born on June 19, 2000.  I counted 19 on the first day although by the next day she was down to 17.  She successfully raised 17 healthy babies.  The mother was a poorly marked Black Tortoiseshell.  The father was a Black Dominant Spot.
